A bunch of brief blurbs on miscellaneous matter; everyday things I learn, things I know but often forget, and anything else I care to write down. They’re not always definitive nor novel but hopefully helpful or at least interesting.

This collection began as a New Year’s resolution inspired by a similar resource by Josh Branchaud from which this project gets its name and the fantastic Learn in Public essay by Shawn Wang.
